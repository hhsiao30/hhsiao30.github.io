Hao-Hsiang Hsiao

Member of Technical Staff, Ricursive Intelligence
Ph.D. Candidate, Georgia Institute of Technology

I am a Member of Technical Staff at Ricursive Intelligence, while completing my Ph.D. at the Georgia Tech Computer-Aided Design Lab (GTCAD) under the supervision of Prof. Sung Kyu Lim.

My research advances Electronic Design Automation (EDA) for 2D and 3D integrated circuits by combining machine learning, LLM-driven optimization, and accelerated computing — toward faster, smarter physical design flows. I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University and my M.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the University of California, Irvine.
