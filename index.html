✉ thsiao@gatech.edu LinkedIn Google Scholar

I am a Member of Technical Staff at Ricursive Intelligence, building agentic AI systems for chip design, while completing my Ph.D. at the Georgia Tech Computer-Aided Design Lab (GTCAD) under the supervision of Prof. Sung Kyu Lim.

My research focuses on leveraging machine learning–driven techniques to enhance Electronic Design Automation (EDA) flows for both 2D and 3D integrated circuits. I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University and my M.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the University of California, Irvine.
